Your Appointment Guide

1 Before Your Appointment

  • Prepare your questions in advance
  • Gather relevant documents (resume, certificates)
  • Test your internet connection and webcam

2 When Your Appointment Starts

  • Join 5 minutes early using the Zoom link in your email
  • Have a notepad ready for important notes
  • Ensure you're in a quiet, well-lit space
