Who We Are

At Pleroma Travel Agency, we provide enriching packages that offer fellow Ghanaians the chance to travel and work abroad.

We are passionate about helping you obtain personal and professional growth through work abroad experiences. Our team coordinates every aspect of the work and travel process for Ghanaians across the country, from the moment you decide to leave, throughout your stay, until you return home.

We provide safe, enriching and beneficial experiences that will remain with you for a lifetime.
